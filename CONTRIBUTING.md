# Contributing to gslb_rust

Thank you for your interest in `gslb_rust`. 

At this stage, the project is in its early development phases and is maintained as a personal project. As such, **I am not currently accepting external contributions, Pull Requests, or feature requests.**

If you find the project useful, feel free to fork it for your own personal use as permitted by the MIT License. I may open the project for collaboration in the future as it reaches a more stable version (currently v1.1.0).
